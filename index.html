Infrastructure

Infrastructure projects aim to provide the region with the infrastructure needed to supply the region with basic necessities such as schools, irrigation systems, wells, dispensaries and electric grids.

Systemic

Systemic projects aim at addressing gaps in the local administrative, commercial, social and/or political system. Projects in this category can be everything from organizing farmers in trade groups to increase their collective bargaining power to mobilize women to take a larger part in local politics. 

Knowledge

Knowledge based projects look to address knowledge gaps in our key strategic areas. These projects can range from our vocational training centers to educating local communities on their legal rights or educating communities on nutritional needs of young children.
